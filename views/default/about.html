What is techclub?

A user-driven tech news aggregator.


How does it work?

Post a link, ask a question, or just say anything that would be of interest to your tech buddies.

Submissions and comments can be upvoted, and are ranked according to freshness and popularity.

Based on their upvoted submissions or comments, each user accumulates points, which reflects their status in the board.


How do I qualify to join?

  1. You are human (or a convincing enough bot)
  2. Have some form of intellectual curiosity
  3. All the three
